XYZ Bank — Policies and General Information

1. Account Types and Minimum Balance
XYZ Bank offers three types of savings accounts.
Regular Savings Account requires a minimum balance of 1000 rupees per month.
Premium Savings Account requires a minimum balance of 5000 rupees per month.
Zero Balance Account has no minimum balance requirement and is available for students and senior citizens.
If the minimum balance is not maintained in a Regular or Premium account, a penalty of 200 rupees per month is charged.

2. EMI and Loan Repayment
EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment.
EMI is automatically deducted from the customer's account on the due date every month.
The due date for EMI is the 5th of every month by default.
If the customer's account does not have sufficient balance on the due date, the EMI bounce charge is 500 rupees per instance.
Customers can prepay their loan partially or fully at any time without any prepayment penalty.
To change the EMI due date, the customer must visit the branch or raise a request through mobile banking.
If a customer misses 3 consecutive EMI payments, the loan account is flagged as NPA (Non Performing Asset) and the case is escalated to the collections team.

3. Interest Rates
Savings account interest rate is 3.5 percent per annum, credited quarterly.
Fixed Deposit interest rates vary by tenure.
FD for 6 months gives 5.5 percent per annum.
FD for 1 year gives 6.5 percent per annum.
FD for 2 years gives 7.0 percent per annum.
FD for 3 years and above gives 7.5 percent per annum.
Senior citizens get an additional 0.5 percent on all FD rates.
Home loan interest rate starts at 8.5 percent per annum.
Personal loan interest rate starts at 11 percent per annum.
Car loan interest rate starts at 9 percent per annum.

4. Late Payment Charges
Credit card late payment fee is 500 rupees if the minimum due is not paid by the due date.
Loan EMI late payment charge is 2 percent of the overdue EMI amount per month.
Minimum payment due on credit card is 5 percent of the total outstanding balance.
If the credit card outstanding is not paid for 90 days, the card is blocked automatically.

5. Internet and Mobile Banking
To activate internet banking, the customer must visit the branch with their account number and registered mobile number.
Mobile banking can be activated directly through the XYZ Bank app using the debit card number and ATM PIN.
Daily transaction limit on mobile banking is 1 lakh rupees by default.
The customer can increase the daily limit up to 5 lakh rupees by submitting a request through the branch.
If internet banking password is forgotten, it can be reset through the app using OTP on registered mobile number.
Three consecutive wrong password attempts will lock the internet banking account.
To unlock, customer must call the helpline at 1800-XYZ-BANK or visit the branch.

6. Debit and Credit Card
Debit card is issued free of cost with every savings account.
Annual fee for a basic credit card is 500 rupees, waived if annual spending exceeds 50000 rupees.
Lost or stolen card must be reported immediately by calling 1800-XYZ-BANK or through mobile banking.
Card will be blocked within 2 minutes of reporting.
Replacement card is issued within 7 working days.
International transactions on debit card are disabled by default and can be enabled through mobile banking.
ATM withdrawal limit is 25000 rupees per day.
POS transaction limit is 1 lakh rupees per day.

7. Fixed Deposits
Minimum amount to open an FD is 1000 rupees.
Maximum tenure for an FD is 10 years.
FD can be broken before maturity with a penalty of 1 percent on the applicable interest rate.
Tax Saving FD has a lock in period of 5 years and cannot be broken before maturity.
Interest on FD above 40000 rupees per year is subject to TDS at 10 percent.
Form 15G or 15H can be submitted to avoid TDS if total income is below taxable limit.

8. Fund Transfers
IMPS transfers are available 24 hours and are instant.
NEFT transfers are processed in batches every 30 minutes on working days.
RTGS is available for transfers above 2 lakh rupees and is processed instantly during banking hours.
Daily IMPS limit is 2 lakh rupees.
First time transfer to a new beneficiary has a cooling period of 4 hours for amounts above 25000 rupees.
UPI transactions are limited to 1 lakh rupees per transaction.

9. KYC and Account Opening
KYC documents required are Aadhaar card and PAN card.
Account can be opened online through the XYZ Bank website or app without visiting the branch.
Video KYC is available for fully digital account opening.
If KYC is not updated every 2 years, the account is frozen for debit transactions.
To update KYC, customer can visit the branch or upload documents through mobile banking.

10. Grievance and Escalation
Customer can raise a complaint through the helpline 1800-XYZ-BANK, mobile banking, or by visiting the branch.
Complaints are resolved within 7 working days.
If the complaint is not resolved within 7 working days, the customer can escalate to the Banking Ombudsman.
The Banking Ombudsman portal is cms.rbi.org.in.
Compensation of 100 rupees per day is paid if the complaint resolution crosses the 7 working day limit due to bank error.
